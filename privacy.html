Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how Blinky collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Blinky collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website and mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as name, phone number, and email.
  • Delivery information including addresses and locations.
  • Payment-related data processed through secure providers.
  • Device and usage data to improve app performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To communicate order updates and support requests.
  • To improve our services and user experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Sharing of Information

Blinky does not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Merchants and delivery partners to fulfill orders.
  • Payment service providers for transaction processing.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Certain requests may be subject to legal limitations.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Blinky may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance website functionality and analyze usage patterns.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. Blinky is not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the service indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@blinkylb.com.
